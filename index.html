Alejandra Silva

The Harris School of Public Policy

My name is Alejandra Silva, and I’m a Master of Public Policy (MPP) student at the University of Chicago’s Harris School of Public Policy. Originally from Peru, I hold a B.S. in Economics and have been a certified Financial Risk Manager (FRM) since 2022.

I’ve worked across government agencies, research centers, and international development institutions—including the Financial Stability Authority of Peru, the Illinois State Treasurer’s Office, NORC at the University of Chicago, and the Cambridge SupTech Lab.

My work focuses on promoting economic mobility and financial inclusion through the use of data and technology. I’ve contributed to projects involving digital financial supervision, public savings tools, and equity-focused policy research at both local and global levels.
